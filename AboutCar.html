Automatic Mode Specifications

  1. 1. The staff checks the capacity of the disinfectant solution.
  2. 2. The staff sets disinfection tasks.
  3. - Cruise Mode: total disinfection time，driving path and disinfection method
  4. - Fixed-point Mode: single point/multi-point disinfection and disinfection method
  5. 3. Puductor 2 automatically reaches the initial point of work.
  6. 4. Puductor 2 begins to count down, and notifies people to stay away from the disinfection areas.
  7. 5. Puductor 2 completes the task as programmed or the staff terminates the task remotely.
  8. 6. Puductor 2 automatically returns to the charging dock.

Manual Mode Specifications

  1. 1. The staff manually pushes the Puductor 2 to the disinfection point.
  2. 2. The staff checks the capacity of the disinfectant solution.
  3. 3. The staff sets disinfection tasks (total disinfection time, disinfection method).
  4. 4. Puductor2 begins to count down, and notifies people to stay away from the disinfection areas.
  5. 5. Puductor 2 completes the task as programmed or the staff terminates the task remotely.
  6. 6. The staff manually pushes Puductor 2 back to the charging dock.
